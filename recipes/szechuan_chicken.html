Szechuan Chicken

A simple Szechuan Chicken, Peppers, and Peas on Rice recipe. The total cook time for this recipe will be approximately 40 mins.

Steps

  1. Place rice in sauce pan and cover with water. Mix with fingers until water is cloudy.
  2. Mix remiaing salt, chicken breasts, and cornstarch into bowl.
  3. Set wok or large skillet over high heat and add oil. Add ingredients.
  4. Whisk soy sauce, rice wine vinergar, garlic, sugar, and chili sauce together.
  5. Divide rice among 4 plates. Top with chicken and sauce. Garnish with green onions.
